HAMILTON: AN AMERICAN MUSICAL

What is Hamilton?

"Hamilton: An American Musical" is a popular Broadway musical created by Lin-Manuel Miranda. It premiered on Broadway in 2015 and quickly gained widespread acclaim and popularity. The musical tells the story of Alexander Hamilton, one of the Founding Fathers of the United States, using a blend of hip-hop, R&B, and traditional musical theater styles.

Hamilton at The White House

Lin-Manuel Miranda Performs at the White House Poetry Jam

Lin-Manuel Miranda first publicly discussed his concept for "Hamilton: An American Musical" at the White House during a special event called the "White House Evening of Poetry, Music, and the Spoken Word" on May 12, 2009. This event was hosted by then-First Lady Michelle Obama and was part of the Obama administration's efforts to celebrate and promote the arts and culture.

Hamilton cast performs "Alexander Hamilton" at White House

The Hamilton Cast's performance at The White House was a notable and historic event that took place on March 14, 2016. This performance was a special one-time event, and it featured the original cast of the Broadway musical "Hamilton: An American Musical" performing selections from the show for then-President Barack Obama and First Lady Michelle Obama, as well as a group of students.
